A Worldly Detox

Cleansing of the Soul

Detoxification of a Muslim

“Allah will not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves.”

Most of us spend countless hours using our computer, ipads, phones whenever we get the chance to. We are so quick in planning when we will get to watch episodes of our favorite shows that we missed for the past month. During these wasteful times in our life we forget about Allah. I call this the disease of technology. Think about how much technology has influenced us in the matter of years. We are now so caught up with social media sites more than Allah himself. These are some of the ways you can detoxify yourself from the disease of technology.

Pray on Time

  • Instead of finishing your essay (which is of course what you were doing) and stalling time, and telling yourself “it’s ok there's 3 more hours to pray Asr” will end up making you miss all your prayers and just overall, making you feel regretful. Praying will only take 4-7 min, which means praying 5 times a day is like spending 20-35 min of your day making Allah happy, your parents, and yourself happy.
