Welcome to the Brooklyn Tech Muslim Student Association

The Brooklyn Tech MSA is an organization where any Brooklyn Tech student or teacher is welcome to come learn about Islam.

Our MSA aims to provide a safe, comfortable, and accepting social atmosphere for students to destress after a week of working and studying, to rejuvinate for the week to come. In such a large school like Tech, it is imperative to have an environment where we can come together to get to know each other, support each other, and comfort each other.

We meet every Thursday in Room 2N6 after 10th period.
